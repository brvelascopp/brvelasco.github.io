Bon Velasco

The author of the satirical essay is Bon Romer P. Velasco. His motivation for this satirical essay came from the situation of today's time. During this pandemic, he realized just how incompetent this
country's government is. He is just a grade 9 high school student studying at the Philippine Science High School Central Luzon Campus. Because of the pandemic, he along with his classmates have to join
online classes because of the quaratine. It was at this time when he realized how bad the government deals with this pandemic, causing him to write a satirical essay. about the government's incompetence.
